Privacy Policy

How WayofMint collects, uses, and protects information in connection with our services and advertising platform integrations.

Effective Date: March 12, 2026

WayofMint ("we," "us," or "our") is a creative agency providing brand strategy, web design, digital advertising, and social media management services. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our services, including our use of the Meta (Facebook) Marketing API through our application, "WayofMint Automation."

Information We Collect

WayofMint accesses the Meta Marketing API to manage advertising campaigns on behalf of our agency clients. The information we access includes:

  • Ad account data (campaign names, budgets, schedules, and performance metrics)
  • Advertising insights and analytics (impressions, reach, clicks, conversions)
  • Ad creative assets (images, copy, and targeting configurations)
  • Business account information necessary for campaign management

We do not collect personal data from end users who view or interact with advertisements managed through our platform. We do not access personal profiles, friend lists, private messages, or any personal information of individual Facebook or Instagram users.

How We Use Information

All data accessed through the Meta Marketing API is used strictly for the purpose of managing and optimizing advertising campaigns for our agency clients. Specifically, we use this information to:

  • Create, manage, and monitor advertising campaigns
  • Analyze campaign performance and generate reports for clients
  • Optimize ad spend and targeting to improve campaign results
  • Provide strategic advertising recommendations to our clients

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share any data obtained through the Meta Marketing API with third parties. Campaign data and performance information is only shared with the specific client whose ad account the data pertains to.

We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, or valid legal process, or if necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of WayofMint, our clients, or others.

Data Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the information we access and process. These measures include:

  • Secure storage of API credentials and access tokens
  • Restricted access to advertising accounts on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular review of access permissions and security practices
  • Use of encrypted connections for all API communications

While no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect all information under our control.

Data Retention

We retain campaign data and performance metrics only for as long as necessary to provide our services to the respective client. When a client relationship ends, we delete or anonymize all associated advertising data within a reasonable timeframe, unless retention is required by law.

Your Rights

If you are a client of WayofMint, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the advertising data we manage on your behalf
  • Request correction of inaccurate data in your advertising accounts
  • Request deletion of your data upon termination of our services
  • Revoke our access to your Meta advertising accounts at any time

If you believe your personal data has been processed through our advertising services and wish to exercise any data protection rights, please contact us using the information below.

Third-Party Platforms

Our services integrate with Meta (Facebook/Instagram) and other advertising platforms. Your use of these platforms is governed by their respective privacy policies. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party platforms connected to our services.
